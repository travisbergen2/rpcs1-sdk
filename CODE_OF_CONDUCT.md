# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

RPCS-1 SDK is a small project, but contributors should still be able to participate without being
harassed, insulted, or dragged into bad-faith arguments.

Please be direct, kind, and technical. Critique ideas and code, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic arguments after maintainers ask for the thread to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; reject pull requests; or block users
who violate this standard.
